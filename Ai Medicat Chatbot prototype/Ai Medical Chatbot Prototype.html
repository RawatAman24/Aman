THE PATHWAY

Three steps, same order every time.

Triage only works if it's consistent. Pulse always moves through the same sequence, so the tag at the end means the same thing every time.

01

Describe

You describe the symptom naturally. No forms, no dropdowns — Pulse extracts onset, severity, and location from plain sentences.

02

Clarify

Pulse asks one to three targeted follow-ups, mirroring the red-flag questions a triage nurse is trained to ask, to rule the serious causes in or out.

03

Route

You get one of three tags — self-care, see a doctor, or seek ER — plus the specific reason, so the advice never feels like a black box.

WHY YOU CAN TRUST THE TAG

Built to defer, not to guess.

Hard stop

Red-flag symptoms always win

Chest pain, stroke signs, breathing trouble, or anything else on Pulse's red-flag list short-circuits the conversation and returns an ER tag immediately — no further chat needed.

Clinician-reviewed

Every pathway is written with physicians

The 380+ symptom pathways are drafted and periodically audited by licensed clinicians against published triage guidelines, not inferred purely from general conversation.

Private by design

Sessions are de-identified

Chat sessions aren't tied to a name unless you create an account. Health data in transit and at rest is encrypted end to end.

Plain limits

Pulse says what it isn't

No prescriptions, no diagnoses, no lab interpretation. Pulse's job is one decision: what should you do in the next hour.

INSIDE THE APP

Everything sits around that one triage decision.

01 · SYMPTOM CHECKER

Guided intake

Conversational, not a form — Pulse follows up the way a nurse would, not with a static checklist.

02 · CARE LOCATOR

Nearest right-fit care

When the tag is "see a doctor," Pulse surfaces same-week clinic slots near you, ranked by wait time.

03 · MED REMINDERS

Dosage nudges

Set a course of medication once; Pulse reminds you on schedule and flags missed-dose patterns.

04 · REPORT READER

Plain-language labs

Upload a lab or scan report; Pulse explains what each flagged value generally means before your follow-up visit.

05 · MULTILINGUAL

19 languages

Full triage pathways run natively in 19 languages, not machine-translated after the fact.

06 · CARE HISTORY

A timeline, not a log file

Past sessions build a simple timeline you can share with a new doctor in one tap.
